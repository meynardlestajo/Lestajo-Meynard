Courses Offered

Business Administration

This course focuses on principles of management, finance, marketing, and other aspects of business administration.

Education

This course covers theories and practices in education, including teaching methodologies and educational psychology.

Hospitality Management

This course focuses on managing hotels, restaurants, and other hospitality establishments, covering areas such as customer service, operations, and marketing.

Information Technology

This course covers various aspects of IT, including networking, database management, cybersecurity, and software development.

Midwifery

This course focuses on the care of pregnant women, childbirth, and postpartum care, as well as newborn care and family planning.

Accountancy

This course covers principles of accounting, financial reporting, auditing, and taxation, preparing students for careers as accountants.

Accounting Information System

This course focuses on the design, implementation, and management of accounting information systems, integrating accounting principles with information technology.
